All About Problems

Problematic Men (Hangul: 문제적남자) is a 2015 South Korean television program starring Jun Hyun-moo, Ha Seok-jin, Kim Ji-seok, Lee Jang-won, Tyler Rasch, and Park Kyung. It airs on tvN on Sunday at 22:50 (KST) beginning February 26, 2015.[1][2] Traditionally, the cast is given clues to guess the identity of the episode's guests, and once the guests appear and are introduced, they and the cast are given a set of puzzles to solve.
